tibs recipes and ingredient

tibs is a traditional food that is eaten in most part of ethiopia

recipes

  1. stir-fry the onion with out the butter
  2. add the meat
  3. add the butter and stir-fry for some minutes
  4. add niter kibbeh when the meat is browen
  5. add the tomatoes,tossegn and wine if you have and cook for a minute or two
  6. done now serve with injera

ingredient

  • onion
  • mitmita
  • meat
  • tossegn
  • tomato
